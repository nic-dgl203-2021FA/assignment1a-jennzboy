Social Good Poster

I created this poster for an assignment in my DGL 102 class, Graphic Design Foundations. The purpose of this assignment was to create a poster inspired by a designer of your choice, about a social cause to inspire action. This poster was inspired by the work of Kel Lauren, and I chose the social cause defund the police, because of the problem of police brutality throughout the world.

Distillery Advert

I created this advert for an assignment in my DGL 112 Typography class. The purpose of this assignment was to create a wordmark for a distillery brand using negative space, and an advert with a persuasive message about the brands unique selling points. I created the brand Crescent Moon Distillery, and used crescent moons in place of the o's in 'moon' to show use of negative space. The goal of the advert was to show that the distillery used locally sourced, organic ingredients in their liquor, and to showcase that with a refreshing cocktail illustration.
